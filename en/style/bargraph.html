Bar Graph

Default

Bar Graph is a style implemented by inheriting from a group and is an ordinary bar graph.

A graph is comprised of title, line and data. 

<div class="bargraph" style="width: 100%;">
	<div style="width: 30%">
		<div class="title">Front</div>
		<div class="info">;
			<div class="line"> </div>
			<div class="data">3.0</div>
		</div>
	</div>
	<div class="active" style="width: 30%">
		<div class="title">Network</div>
		<div class="info">;
			<div class="line"> </div>
			<div class="data">3.0</div>
		</div>
	</div>
	<div style="width: 20%">
		<div class="title">DNS</div>
		<div class="info">;
			<div class="line"> </div>
			<div class="data">2.0</div>
		</div>
	</div>
	<div style="width: 20%">
		<div class="title">Server</div>
		<div class="info">;
			<div class="line"> </div>
			<div class="data">2.0</div>
		</div>
	</div>
</div>
